# Privacy Policy for Slightly Epic Radio

_Last updated: April 11, 2026_

Slightly Epic Radio ("the app") is a free internet radio player for Android. This policy explains what information the app does and does not handle.

## Information we collect

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.** The app has no accounts, no sign-in, no analytics, no advertising SDKs, and no crash-reporting services.

The only data the app persists is stored locally on your device using Android's DataStore:

- The ID of the last radio station you played, so the app can restore it the next time you open it.

This information never leaves your device. Uninstalling the app removes it.

## Network activity

To play radio, the app connects directly to third-party Icecast/Shoutcast streaming servers operated by independent broadcasters. These connections are required for the core functionality of the app. When you play a station, the app may also fetch publicly available "now playing" metadata from the same servers.

We do not proxy, log, or record any of this traffic. The third-party stream operators may keep their own connection logs according to their own policies, which are outside our control. If you are concerned about what a specific station's operator logs, please consult that station's website.

## Third-party content

The radio stations featured in the app are operated by independent third parties. The app does not produce, curate, moderate, or control the audio content that those stations broadcast. Programming — including music, talk, advertising, and any explicit or adult language — is the sole responsibility of the individual station operators. If you encounter content you find objectionable, please contact that station directly or stop playback.

## Permissions

The app requests the following Android permissions, used only for the purposes described:

- **Internet** — to connect to radio streams.
- **Foreground service / media playback** — to keep audio playing while the app is in the background and show playback controls in the notification shade.
- **Post notifications** (Android 13+) — to display the media playback notification.

None of these permissions are used to collect information about you.

## Children's privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from anyone. Because the app streams third-party radio that may include explicit language, parents should supervise use by younger listeners.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at the same URL with a new "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
